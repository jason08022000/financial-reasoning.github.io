Effective Annual Rate (EAR) Calculation

The effective annual rate (EAR) is calculated as follows:

$$\text{EAR} = (1 + \text{Periodic interest rate})^n - 1$$

$$\text{EAR} = (1 + 0.03/365)^{365} - 1$$

$$\text{EAR} = (1.03045) - 1 = 0.030453 \approx 3.0453\%$$
